About Us

Welcome to Our Church Family

Glory and Shine Evangelical Mission is a vibrant community of believers located in Oke Ejigbo, Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria.

We are a church committed to the gospel of Jesus Christ, dedicated to seeing lives transformed through Salvation, Healing, and Miracles. Our doors are open to everyone seeking a deeper relationship with God and a loving church family.

Founded on the timeless truth of Hebrews 13:8 — "Jesus Christ the same yesterday, and today, and forever" — we believe in the unchanging power of God to transform lives, heal the sick, and perform miracles today just as He did in biblical times.

Our Services

Join Us in Worship

Experience the presence of God with us at any of our weekly services

Sunday Worship Service

Every Sunday @ 9:00 AM

Our main gathering where we come together as a family to worship God, hear His Word preached, and experience His presence. All are welcome!

  • Praise & Worship
  • Powerful Sermon
  • Prayer & Altar Call
  • Children's Church

Bible Study

Every Tuesday

Dive deeper into God's Word with us. Our Bible Study sessions help you understand scripture and apply it to your daily life.

  • Verse-by-Verse Study
  • Group Discussion
  • Practical Application

Prayer Meeting

Every Thursday

Join us for powerful corporate prayers where we intercede for our families, community, nation, and the world.

  • Corporate Intercession
  • Praise & Worship
  • Testimonies
Our Leaders

Meet Our Pastors

Dedicated servants of God leading our congregation with love and wisdom

Pastor Timothy Kolawole

Senior Pastor / General Overseer

Pastor Timothy Kolawole is a dedicated man of God with a passion for souls and a heart for revival. Under his leadership, Glory and Shine Evangelical Mission continues to impact lives through the message of Salvation, Healing, and Miracles.

Pastor Mrs. Bolanle Kolawole

Co-Pastor / Women's Ministry Leader

Pastor Mrs. Bolanle Kolawole serves alongside her husband with grace and dedication. She is instrumental in the women's ministry and plays a vital role in the spiritual growth and nurturing of the congregation.
